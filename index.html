About DHRMS

The Digital Health Records Management System (DHRMS) is designed specifically for migrant workers in Kerala, addressing the critical need for comprehensive health record management. Our system ensures fair and impartial healthcare access while supporting public health surveillance and disease prevention.

Built with modern web technologies and aligned with Sustainable Development Goals, DHRMS provides a secure, accessible platform for managing health records across different user roles including migrant workers, healthcare providers, and system administrators.
